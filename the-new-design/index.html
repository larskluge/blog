A New Look

Jan 12th, 2014

When we launched CrowdCurity in September 2013, we launched with the goal to bring bug bounty programs to the market as quickly as possible. In order to achieve that goal, we originally used Bootstrap for our first design layout, though we know that professional, unique design is an important element in launching an online business.

Today, we are proud to release a newly-designed website that we hope will help us build trust with trust and delight our users. We have worked hard on our newest design, and couldn’t be more excited to launch a redesigned site experience that will take CrowdCurity to the next level.

Design Thinking For our new design we teamed up with the awesome guys from El Passion, a bitcoin-friendly design firm from Warsaw, Poland. Through an iterative design process, we were able to define our visual identity including a color scheme, a new layout and illustrations. Working with El Passion helped us accelerate the design process and ensured that our design is optimized for both web and mobile.

Color Because color is a substantial part of any design, we put serious thought into the colors we use on the CrowdCurity site. The dark blue color we chose during our design process was chosen as a signal of trust, and the cyan represents the modernity and disruption crowdsourced web security brings to traditional security services. Simplicity + Usability Delivering a platform which is simple and intuitive for our users is crucial in everything we do. In our new design, we have employed streamlined content and a focus on highlighting key buttons and information for the user. Additionally, the font type we chose (a light version of Lato) is modern, friendly, easily readable in places like our Terms and Conditions, a particularly text-dense part of our site. Only the beginning Though our new design is now live on public-facing CrowdCurity pages, we will continue the work on updating the rest of the site to create the best user experience possible. On a side note, we are proud to continue our Scandinavian design heritage of minimalism and cleanliness! : ) Take a look at our new site, and tell us what you think!
