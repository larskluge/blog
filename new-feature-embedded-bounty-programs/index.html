New feature: Embedded bounty programs

Jul 3rd, 2014

Today, we are excited to explore a recent feature release, embedded bug bounty programs, that allow businesses to further build trust with their users. This feature will allow businesses to customize their bounty programs in their brand colors directly on their own websites, and open a new channel of communication for vulnerability disclosure through live, public bounty programs hosted through CrowdCurity.

The option to embed bounty programs is available to all programs that are live and open to the public through the CrowdCurity platform. To add your program to your business’s site:

1— Login to your CrowdCurity account, and navigate to your Program Settings located on the right hand side of your Dashboard.

2— In program settings, navigate to “Embed Program” on the left menu. When the page opens, the embed code will appear with options that will allow you to customize and preview your program before adding it to your site.

3— Once the embed is ready, copy the embed code from the top of the page to your clipboard, and paste it to the code for appropriate page on your website.

In addition to embedding a rewards programs, businesses with live, public bug bounty programs may also be eligible to display the “Secured by CrowdCurity” seal on their sites to show their commitment to web security. Learn more about the CrowdCurity seal here or here.
