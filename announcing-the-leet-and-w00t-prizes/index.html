Announcing the LEET and W00t Prizes!

Nov 14th, 2014

We at CrowdCurity love our researchers. With all their hard work, our platform has enabled them to earn more than 1000 bounties! This means we are almost to our 1337th bounty, and that number has special significance to our researchers and us.

For the few that aren’t aware, 1337 in Leetspeak means Leet, short for elite. First written in the 1980s on BBS’s, it continues to be used to denote awesomeness; especially in tech, gaming, and security circles.

With that in mind, we will be awarding two prizes in anticipation of CrowdCurity’s 1337th bounty rewarded:

The W00t Prize Between now and bounty 1337, we are awarding an additional $100 randomly to one bounty!

The LEET Prize We are doubling the 1337th bounty when the tester collects it!

There’s no time to lose! Sign up here to test, or check out our clients page to see who you can test. As of this posting we have had 1258 bounties rewarded, so it won’t be long now. Keep an eye on our twitter feed for updates on the chase.

Happy Hunting!
