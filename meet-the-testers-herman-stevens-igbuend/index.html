Meet the testers: Herman Stevens

May 6th, 2014

In the second interview of our “Meet the Testers” series, CrowdCurity co-founder Esben Friis-Jensen spent some time via Google Hangout with Herman Stevens (a.k.a. Igbuend), a security researcher based in Singapore. Herman has more than 15 years of security experience, and has recently decided to try out bug bounties to get new challenges with great success.

Do you enjoy the challenges posed by participating in bug bounty programs? Watch the interview and tell us what you think in the comments.
