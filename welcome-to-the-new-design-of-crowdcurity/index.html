Hello, welcome to the new design of Crowdcurity!

Jan 7th, 2015

During the last couple of months we have worked hard to redesign the face of Crowdcurity. Today is the when it all comes out – today you all get to delve into it.

All of our public facing pages have been reworked, redesigned, and changed to better deliver the message about our mission to deliver the world's best online security network. It has been a tonne of hard work a process of narrowing in, optimizing, and polishing every single bit of our site, but we think the result is nothing less than amazing. We hope you like it too.

When you sign in, you are taken to Crowdcurity Central, where you will find your dashboard and menu in a refreshed, eye pleasing layout. The UX is kept frictionless and recognizable with a couple of improvements, but in an all new suit. Both security researchers and security-aware companies will still feel right at home.

We want to send a special thanks to Matt, Lars, and Mariusz (you know who you are!), who helped put all this together. Design, user experience is never over and we will continue to iterate, tweak, fix bugs, and add deligthfulness going forward. Let us know of any bugs – and should you find a security vulnerability in our platform, report it and be rewarded: https://www.crowdcurity.com/crowdcurity!

Welcome to the world's best crowdsourced security.

Welcome to the new Crowdcurity!
