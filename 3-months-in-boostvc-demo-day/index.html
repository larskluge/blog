3 Months in Boost.vc - Demo Day

Feb 10th, 2014

This Tuesday, (February 11th) we will be demoing CrowdCurity in Menlo Park, right here in Silicon Valley. The demo day marks 3 months in the Boost.vc startup accelerator in San Mateo in the heart of Silicon Valley. We have been working our asses off, in a basement in Boost, along with 15 other awesome startups in the third Boost.vc batch.

So far, this has been the most exciting journey. We’re facing daily challenges from everywhere and overcome each challenge one-by-one in an extreme learning curve. Hard work, fast learning, saying no to unimportant tasks, and reaching out to our network have gotten us further than we could have imagined. That, and endless commitment.

To put it in numbers, since we arrived in Silicon Valley we have had a 80-90% month-over-month growth:

  • 5X clients running paid reward programs
  • 5X security testers
  • 8X revenue

We have also seen a 25% increase in our team, thanks to the addition of our great design intern Kevin.

It looks like we’re going to stick around in the Valley a while longer, so if you ever see someone wearing a CrowdCurity t-shirt please stop us and say hi – we’d be happy to talk. We’ll all be wearing our t-shirts for our demo day this Tuesday.

Hope to see you there!
